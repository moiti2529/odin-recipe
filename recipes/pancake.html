Fulfilling pancakes recipe

Description

Custard type pancake served warm with maple syrup that is sure to become a family favorite - our family has been enjoying this for many years.

Information

Prep time: 15min

Cook time: 35min

Total time: 50min

Serving: 6

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Heavy cream
  • Magarine

How to make Austrian pancakes

  1. Preheat the oven
  2. Make the batter and pour in a skillet
  3. Bake in the oven for 35min. Remove and let sit for 5min and enjoy
