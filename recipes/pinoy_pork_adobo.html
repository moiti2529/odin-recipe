Melts in your mouth pinoy adobo pork recipe

Description

This meaty savory dish will melt as soon as it goes inside your mouth and brings about a burst of flavor. It sure is one of the most infamous rice thieves!

Information

Prep time: 15min

Cook time: 1hr 15min

Total time: 2hrs 30

Servings: 6

Additional time: 1hr

\

Ingredients

  • Prok belly
  • Seasonings
  • Red onion
  • Diced apple
  • Sparkling lemonade
  • Bell pepper, green and red
  • Cornstarch
  • Garlic
  • Coconut oil
  • Ginger

How to make delicious, authentic pork adobo

  1. Marinate the pork
  2. Saute the garlic, onion, ginger to make the base sauce
  3. Put in the pork and simmer til tender
